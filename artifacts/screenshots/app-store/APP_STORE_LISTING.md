## Description

**Find Your Perfect Hat**

Moon Ridge Hat Finder helps you discover luxury western and dress hats tailored to your style and head shape — powered by the Moon Ridge catalog.

**How it works**
• Choose hat type — felt, straw, ball cap, and more
• Pick your style — Western, City, or Outdoor
• Select crown and brim shapes with visual guides
• Filter results by size, color, and style
• Shop matching hats on moonridgecompany.com

**Learn as you go**
• Head shape profile to personalize recommendations
• Crown and brim shape guides with history and famous wearers
• How-to-measure video for hat sizing

Built for Moon Ridge Hats & Heritage — fine hats, honest craftsmanship, and western tradition.
